Terraria Weapons

Not all Weapons listed; suggested ones only.

Order and Obtainment

Warrior Weapons

  • Copper shortsword - your trusty starting weapon Maces are also good at this point (you start with this on spawn)
  • Platinum/Gold Broadsword - very solid starting weapon(obtained from mining gold/platinum bars) The terragrim is also very OP but is very hard to get.
  • Blade of grass/Volcano - While both are very good, Volcano is way better (volcano is from Hellstone bar, a rare mineral, and the blade of grass is made from a bunch of jungle stuff like vines and thorns.)
  • Nights Edge - The best sword pre-hardmode, has a very big sweep (Made from the Volcano, Blade of Grass, Muramasa, and the Lights bane/Blood Butcher)
  • Honorable Pre-hardmode mentions - Dark lance and Sunfury(Chests in hell), Flamarang(hellstone bars and enchanted boomerang)

    Hardmode

  • Frostbrand - A sweat weapon that shoots an ice ball forward, you can use the Titanium Sword too (Dropped from ice mimics which are pretty hard to kill)
  • Excalibur - A really top tier weapon (made from hollowed bars dropped from The Destroyer,The Twins, or Skeletron Prime)
  • True Excalibur/True Nights Edge - One of the best weapons in the game (made from either weapons base form plus a broken hero sword dropped from the solar eclipse both Mothra.)
  • Terrablade - Mixing the True Excalibur and the True nights edge gives you the best weapon before beating the game
  • Honorable Mentions - Vampire knives(Crimson biome chest, really good weapon that heals you),Possesd Hatchet (auto aim dps, from golem) The Zenith, the strongest weapon in the game (mix all important swords together)

Mage Weapons

  • Gem staffs - they just shoot a projectile, nothing special (mix 12 bars of material plus the corresponding gem to make it eg, amethyst staff requires copper and amethyst)
  • Water Bolt - Shoots bouncing projectiles that pack a punch (found on shelves in the dungeon; its a book)
  • Vilethorn/Space gun - Vilethorn shoots a piercing spike (Obtained from breaking shadow orbs), space gun shoots like a gun (Made from Meteortite)
  • Bee Gun - Shoots bees that track your opponent, probably one of the best choices early on (From Queen Bee)
  • Honorable Mentions - Crimson Rod, throws a high dps cloud (From Crimison hearts), Demon Scythe, shoots spinning blades (from demons in hell)

    Hardmode

  • Crstal Serpent/Sky Fracture - Both weapons destroy groups and the crystal serpent can be used for a very long time (from fishing and hallowed materials respectively)
  • Meteor staff - Rains down meteors at a medium pace (made from meteorite and other materials)
  • Blizzard Staff/Razorpine - Rain a storm of icicles on opponents, clears crowds. Shoot a wave of pines (both weapons from the Frost Moon event)
  • Razorblade Typhoon - Literally one of the best mage weapons in the game! (from Duke Fishron)

Summoner Weapons

  • Finch Staff and Leather Whip - Good start using both at once, use the whip after summoning - (both from chests, whip from dessert chests)
  • Hornet Staff and Snapthorn - Tons of poison, snapthorn has auto-swing with poison, and Hornets are ranged (from Queen bee and Jungle Materials respectively)
  • Vampire Frog Staff and Spinal Tap - Both are good weapons, frog staff is one of the best pre-hardmode summons (from Blood Moon fishing and Bones and webs respectively)
  • Imp staff and Spinal tap - Same Whip, better summon that shoots ranged fire balls (staff is made from hellstone)

    Hardmode

  • Spider Staff and Firecracker - fun poison and fire damage together makes a lot of tick damage (From spiders and Wall of Flesh)
  • Optic Staff and Durendal - Optic staff is good when you have many Summon slots (it spawns two for one slot) and the Durendal speeds up with every hit (both made from Mechanical bosses drops)
  • Pygmy Staff and Dark Harvest - Pygmy has good range, while Dark Harvest is slow with massive damage(from Plantera, and The Pumpkin moon event)
  • Raven Staff/Tempest Staff and Morning Star - Tempest staff shoots sharks and morning star does group damage (from Duke Fishron, and Dungeon Enemies)
  • Terraprism and Kaleidoscope - the best summoner weapons together! Kaleidoscope attacks fast, terraprism does way too much damage (From day time Empress Light and normal Empress of Light)

Ranger Weapons

  • Wooden Bow/Flintlock - Both are ok starting weapons. You can also get the undertake from Crimson Hearts (Flintlock is from Gunsmith for 5 gold)
  • Tendon Bow/Minishark - REALLY good weapons, minishark can be upgraded in hardmode (from Crimtane ore and Minishark is from Gunsmith for 35 gold)
  • Bee's Knees/Minishark - Bees knees changes arrows into lines of bees, really good for Wall of Flesh (from Queen Bee)
  • Hellwing bow/Phoenix Blaster - Hellwing bow changes arrows into inaccurate fast firing bats, phoenix blaster just does a lot of damage (Bow from Shadow Chests, Gun is made from the Handgun and Hellstone)

    Hardmode

  • Daedalus Storm Bow/Onyx Blaster - The bow is one of the strongests bows, rains arrows with stars instead of shooting normally, onyx blaster does high impcat damage and shoots a grenade (Bow from hallowed mimics and Gun from boomstick and Dessert Fragments)
  • Hallowed Repeater/Megashark - (make sure to still use Daedalus Bow) Hallowed repeater shoots arrows VERY fast, and the Megashark is the better minishark, shooting fast and hard (from Hallowed bars and souls from mechanical bosses)
  • Chaingun/tsunami - Chaingun is a powerful minigun, nothing further to say about it. The bow shoots a vertical line of arrows (from SANTA-MK1, and Duke Fishron)
  • Vortex Beater/Phantasm - Both are some of the best, vortext beater shoots insanely fast and every 4th bullet shoots a homing explosive, Phantasm shoots arrows, then after a delay, fires them in the same spot (BOTH from the Vortex Pillar.)
