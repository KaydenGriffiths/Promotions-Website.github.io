Terraria's Useful Accesories

Accesories give buffs based on what they are. Each accesory has a difference use for certain builds. A player can use 6 different accesories.

Best Equipment
 Mobility Equipment
  • Wings --> A short burst of flight allows for easy movement; can be obtained pre hardmode and early hardmode easily
  • Heremes/spark/lighting/Terrarian boots --> Allows you to run fast if you walk uniterupted for a period of time.
  • Hooks --> Hooks onto objects for some pretty sweet movement
  • Mounts --> Summons a pet-like thing for you to ride; it makes you zoom
  • Misc --> things like balloons (floatation), flippers (siwmming), Bottled cloud/blizzard (floatation)
Combat Accesories
  • Immunities --> Grants immunity to certain debuffs such as bleeding, poison, and silence.
  • Damage accesories --> Items such as the emblem grant percentille buffs (eg, 15% dmg buff to swords or whatever)
  • Mage accesories --> Accesories that give more Mana (used for magic) or make getting Mana easier
  • Melee accesories --> Gives buffs to swords and usually gives a ton of defence to soak up damage
  • Summoner accesories --> Gives more damage to summoned minions, whip damage/speed, or even the amount of sentries and minions
  • Ranger accesories --> Grants more ammo conservation, damage, and sometimes critical strike chance
  • Defensive accesories --> Grants the user a higher defence stat which allows them to tank more hits and survive longer
  • Regenerative accesories --> Makes your max hp go up, or the rate you recieve health fasterD
Other Accesories
  • Fishing --> Fishing tools are exactly what they sound like, they give a bonus chance to get better fish and more rare fish.
  • Informational Equips --> Displays info based on the tool, can be combined to make the Cellphone which displays everything. (tools can show: nearby enemies and depths)
  • Vanity --> Coats, Developer sets, and some sick shades all of which is designed to make your character look hella good
  • Yo-Yo's --> The most fun melee weapons ever have far too many accesoriess. They make it so you can have extra Counter-Weights while flinging your Yo-Yo
  • Misc Accesories --> Mainly to deal with collecting coins and saving them, giving off light, or changing your vision and what you see
  • Expert-Mode Only --> usually pretty damn strong, the objectively best expert mode ones are the Shield of Chluthu (allows you to dash which is good for dodging) and Worm Scarf which reduces incoming dmg by 17%
